The Story

A Passion Turned
Into A Profession

I got into window tinting because I wanted my own car tinted but couldn't afford to pay someone else. So I bought a couple rolls and figured it out. It wasn't pretty at first, but I kept coming back and kept improving. Eventually I decided to take it seriously and put real time into learning the craft.

Now Oscuro Tintz is built on clean, high-quality installs using premium Hitek film, with attention to detail on every job. What started as a hobby on my own vehicles became a business built on word of mouth and results that speak for themselves.

Every job gets the same treatment, whether it's a daily driver, a weekend build, or a family SUV. No shortcuts, no rushed edges, no leaving a job until it's right.
