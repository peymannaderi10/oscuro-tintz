Ask A Question
How long does a full-vehicle tint take?
Most sedans and small SUVs are done in 2 to 3 hours. Trucks and larger SUVs can run 3 to 4. We give you a real time estimate when you book.
Will the tint bubble or turn purple?
Not on Hitek film. The dyes are stable and the adhesive is engineered to stay clear for the life of the install. That's what the lifetime warranty covers.
How dark can I legally go in California?
Front side windows must allow more than 70% of light through. Rear and rear-side windows can be any darkness. Windshield, top 4 inches only. We follow the law on every install.
How soon can I roll my windows down?
Wait 3 to 5 days. The film needs to fully bond. We send a care card and a follow-up text on day 3 to remind you.
Do you offer mobile installs?
Yes. Driveway, office parking lot, body shop. We bring everything we need and work in any clean, covered space. Yuba and Sutter County, no extra charge.
What if I have old, bad tint already?
We strip it. Removal starts at $50 per window depending on age and adhesive. New install can go on the same day in most cases.
